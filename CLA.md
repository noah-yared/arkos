## Statement of Intent (Non-Binding)

ArkOS is developed as an open-source project with a sustainable long-term model. To be explicit from the beginning:

- **Yes, we retain the right to run a paid hosted service.** We operate a paid hosted service to lower the barrier to entry for users who value ArkOS’s architecture but cannot or do not want to manage GPUs, deployment, upgrades, and uptime themselves. Providing a hosted option allows us to sustain active development, maintenance, and support of the project, while preserving full freedom for anyone to self-host ArkOS and without closing or restricting the open-source core.

- **No, we will not close the open-source core.** The core ArkOS codebase will remain open source, allowing users and contributors to inspect, modify, and run the software independently of any hosted offering.

- **Yes, you can self-host ArkOS indefinitely.** ArkOS may always be self-hosted and run on user-controlled infrastructure without reliance on the hosted service.

These statements describe the project’s current intent and direction.  
They are non-binding and do not modify the legal terms of this Agreement.
